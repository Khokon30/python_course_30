Necessity of Learning English

English is an international language. It is a generally accepted language for maintaining effective communication with the rest of the world.
So English is necessary for all sorts of internatinal commmunication like trade and commerce.
Most of the books of higher studies are written in English. As a result, higher education is almost impossible without good knowledge of English.
English is most helpful to a man during his stay in a foreign country. English is the main or official language of most of the international and many regional organisations.
English is important to work with them. Most of the great works of literature of the world are either written or translated in English.
Only English can help us to know about them. English is unavoidable in medical science, information and tehnology.
Again, proficiency in English is often considered the standard of qualification. English can help us to get good jobs and better salaries.
In fact, English is helpful to us in many ways and we must learn English.
